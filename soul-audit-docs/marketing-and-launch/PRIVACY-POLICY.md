## The Short Version

We collect only what we need to give you a personalized devotional experience. We do not sell your data. We do not share it with advertisers. We use it to help you find content that meets you where you are spiritually.

---

## What We Collect

### Information You Provide

**Email Address**

- Used for account creation via magic link authentication
- Allows you to save your progress across devices
- We may send occasional service-related emails (password resets, major updates)
- We do not send marketing emails unless you opt in

**Soul Audit Responses**

- Your answers to questions like "What are you wrestling with today?"
- Used to match you with devotional series that fit where you are
- Processed by our AI matching system to suggest appropriate content
- Limited to 3 Soul Audits per account

**Preferences**

- Sabbath day preference (Saturday or Sunday)
- Theme preference (light or dark mode)
- Timezone (for day-gating content at 7 AM your time)

**Progress Data**

- Which series you have started
- Which days you have completed
- Where you left off

### Information Collected Automatically

**Basic Analytics**

- Pages visited
- Time spent on site
- Device type and browser
- General location (country/region level, not precise)

We use privacy-respecting analytics (Vercel Analytics or Plausible) that do not track you across the web.

## Data Retention

**Active Accounts**

- Your data is retained as long as your account is active
- "Active" means you have logged in within the past 12 months

**Inactive Accounts**

- If you do not log in for 12 months, we may delete your account and associated data
- We will attempt to notify you by email before deletion

**Deletion Requests**

- You can request deletion at any time (see Your Rights below)
- We will delete your data within 30 days of a verified request

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

**Access Your Data**

- Request a copy of all data we have about you
- Contact us at privacy@wokegod.world

**Correct Your Data**

- Update your email, preferences, or other information in Settings

**Delete Your Data**

- Request complete deletion of your account and all associated data
- Email privacy@wokegod.world with "Delete My Account" in the subject line

**Export Your Data**

- Request a portable copy of your data
- We will provide it in a common format (JSON or CSV)

**Withdraw Consent**

- Stop using the service at any time
- Delete your account to remove all data
