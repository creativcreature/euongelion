**Secondary (Awake pathway):**

- Committed Christians wanting depth
- People in a spiritual rut

**Tertiary (Shepherd pathway):**

- Pastors and ministry leaders
- Small group leaders
- Christian content creators

- Urgent but not aggressive
- Thoughtful, not preachy
- Culturally fluent, not cringe
- Questions over statements

## CONTENT PILLARS

### 1. Wake Up Moments (40%)

Provocative questions that stop the scroll.

- "What has fear taught you to worship?"
- "What would change if you weren't afraid?"
- "When did busyness become your religion?"

### 2. Scripture Depth (30%)

Hebrew/Greek insights that surprise.

- "This word doesn't mean what you think"
- "The hidden meaning in [verse]"
- Etymology graphics

### 3. Real Talk (20%)

Honest, vulnerable, relatable.

- Struggle without performance
- Doubt without despair
- Behind-the-scenes building

### 4. Invitation (10%)

Direct calls to engage.

- "Try the Soul Audit"
- "Start your first series"
- Testimonies from users

## LAUNCH STRATEGY

### Phase 1: Pre-Launch (2-3 weeks before)

**Week -3:**

- Start posting "Wake Up" content
- Build anticipation without explaining everything
- Tease the concept

**Week -2:**

- Introduce EUANGELION name
- Share development journey
- "Something is coming"

**Week -1:**

- Show sneak peeks
- Countdown posts
- Build email waitlist

### Phase 2: Launch Week

**Day 1 (Monday):**

- Announce launch
- Share Soul Audit
- Personal testimony/why

**Day 2-3:**

- User testimonials (if any)
- Feature highlights
- Answer questions

**Day 4-5:**

- Deeper content
- Hebrew word post
- Engagement focus

**Day 6-7:**

- Sabbath-aligned reflection
- Week 1 learnings
- Thank the community

### Phase 3: Post-Launch (Ongoing)

- Consistent daily posting
- User-generated content
- Iteration based on data
- Community cultivation

## SOCIAL MEDIA GUIDELINES

### Do:

- Ask questions
- Share struggle honestly
- Use "we" and "us"
- Respond to every comment
- Admit mistakes publicly

### Don't:

- Preach at people
- Use churchy jargon (for Sleep audience)
- Post just to post
- Ignore negative feedback
- Be performatively vulnerable

### Crisis Protocol:

1. Pause scheduled posts
2. Acknowledge the issue
3. Consult STRATEGIST
4. Respond honestly
5. Learn and adapt
