## How to Use This Bank

Breath prayers are short, rhythmic phrases designed for meditative repetition. Each prayer has an **inhale** and **exhale** component, creating a natural breathing rhythm that anchors the prayer in the body.

**Format:**

- **Inhale:** Receiving truth (typically about God or His character)
- **Exhale:** Responding in faith (typically our posture or surrender)

**Best Practices:**

- Pair with 4-7-8 breathing (inhale 4 counts, hold 7, exhale 8)
- Use as transition between devotional sections
- Offer as daily practice recommendation
- Extract for social sharing (breath prayer cards)

## Usage Notes

### Pairing with Scripture

Each breath prayer category aligns with specific Scripture passages. When using in devotionals:

- Peace/Anxiety: Psalm 46, Philippians 4:6-7, Matthew 6:25-34
- Trust/Doubt: Proverbs 3:5-6, Mark 9:24, Isaiah 55:8-9
- Grief/Loss: Psalm 34:18, 2 Corinthians 1:3-4, John 11:35
- Praise/Gratitude: Psalm 103, Psalm 100, 1 Thessalonians 5:18
- Surrender: Matthew 26:39, Romans 12:1, Luke 9:23
- Identity: Ephesians 1:3-14, Psalm 139, 2 Corinthians 5:17

### Social Sharing Format

When extracting for social cards:

```
Breathe in:
[Inhale phrase]

Breathe out:
[Exhale phrase]
```

### Audio/Visual Pairing

Consider pairing breath prayers with:

- Ambient background sound
- 4-7-8 breathing timer
- Calming visuals (water, light, nature)
- Hebrew/Greek word display of key terms
