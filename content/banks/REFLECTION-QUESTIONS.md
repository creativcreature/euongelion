# EUONGELION Reflection Question Bank

**Version:** 1.0
**Last Updated:** January 17, 2026
**Purpose:** Reusable reflection questions for devotional content, journaling prompts, and social sharing

---

## How to Use This Bank

Reflection questions move readers from passive consumption to active engagement. They create space for the Holy Spirit to personalize Scripture and teaching to each reader's situation.

**Best Practices:**

- Use 2-3 questions per devotional day
- Match question depth to content depth
- Sequence from accessible to challenging
- Avoid compound questions (one question, one focus)
- Extract for social sharing (question cards)

**Question Quality Checklist:**

- [ ] Specific enough to be actionable
- [ ] Personal without being invasive
- [ ] Creates desire to explore more
- [ ] Works for believers AND seekers
- [ ] Avoids insider Christian language

## Identity Questions

Questions that explore who we are in relation to God, others, and ourselves.

### Core Identity

1. What would change if you fully believed you were beloved before you accomplished anything?
2. When did you first realize your identity was more than your achievements?
3. What name have you been calling yourself that God never gave you?
4. If your failures were erased from your record, would you still know who you are?
5. What part of your identity are you most afraid to lose?
6. Who do you become when no one is watching?
7. What masks do you wear in different contexts, and why?
8. If you could only be known for one thing, what would you want it to be?
9. What would your life look like if you lived from acceptance rather than for acceptance?
10. When do you feel most authentically yourself?

### Past and Present

11. What story from your past still defines how you see yourself today?
12. What wound shaped your identity more than you'd like to admit?
13. What parts of your childhood self have you abandoned that you miss?

17. What truth about yourself are you avoiding?

### Image of God

22. What part of bearing God's image feels like a burden?
23. How would you treat yourself differently if you saw yourself as God's artwork?
24. What would it mean to partner with God in forming your character?
25. What divine attribute do you have the hardest time believing could exist in you?
26. How does being made in God's image change how you view others?
27. What gift has God placed in you that you're neglecting?

## Usage Notes

### Pairing with Devotional Content

Match question types to devotional themes:

- Identity questions → Who Am I?, Prodigal series
- Relationship questions → Forgiveness, One Another series
- Purpose questions → Calling, Vocation, Meaning of Life series
- Suffering questions → Grief, Lament, Wilderness series
- Faith questions → Doubt, Apologetics, Foundations series
- Practice questions → Disciplines, Sabbath, Prayer series
- Gratitude questions → Thanksgiving, Abundance series

### Social Sharing Format

```
Today's question:

[Single powerful question]

Sit with this.
```

### Journaling Prompt Format

```
Spend 10 minutes with this question:

[Question]

Write whatever comes to mind. Don't edit.
```

### Discussion Guide Format

```
Discuss together:
1. [Opening question - accessible]
2. [Deeper question - personal]
3. [Application question - actionable]
```
