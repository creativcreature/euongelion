## Introduction

This Cookie Policy explains how EUONGELION ("we," "our," or "us") uses cookies and similar technologies when you use our devotional application and website (collectively, the "Service").

By using the Service, you consent to the use of cookies as described in this policy.

## What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website or use an application. They are widely used to make services work efficiently and provide information to service providers.

### Types of Storage Technologies We Use

- **Cookies:** Small text files stored in your browser
- **Local Storage:** Data stored in your browser's local storage
- **IndexedDB:** Database storage for larger amounts of data
- **Service Workers:** Scripts that enable offline functionality

## How We Use Cookies

### Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the Service to function properly. They cannot be disabled.

| Cookie Name | Purpose                      | Duration |
| ----------- | ---------------------------- | -------- |
| session_id  | Maintains your login session | Session  |
| csrf_token  | Security protection          | Session  |
| auth_token  | Authentication               | 30 days  |

**Purpose:**

- Maintain your logged-in state
- Remember your security preferences
- Enable core functionality

### Functional Cookies

These cookies enable enhanced functionality and personalization.

| Cookie Name  | Purpose                   | Duration |
| ------------ | ------------------------- | -------- |
| preferences  | Stores your app settings  | 1 year   |
| theme        | Remembers dark/light mode | 1 year   |
| reading_font | Stores font preferences   | 1 year   |

**Purpose:**

- Remember your display preferences
- Store reading settings
- Maintain personalization choices

### Analytics Cookies

These cookies help us understand how visitors use the Service.

| Cookie Name    | Purpose                  | Duration |
| -------------- | ------------------------ | -------- |
| \_analytics_id | Anonymous usage tracking | 2 years  |
| \_session      | Session analytics        | Session  |

**Purpose:**

- Understand usage patterns
- Identify popular features
- Improve the Service

### Performance Cookies

These cookies help us monitor and improve Service performance.

| Cookie Name | Purpose             | Duration |
| ----------- | ------------------- | -------- |
| \_perf      | Performance metrics | Session  |
| \_error     | Error tracking      | 7 days   |

**Purpose:**

- Monitor page load times
- Track and resolve errors
- Optimize performance

## Progressive Web App Storage

As a PWA, EUONGELION uses additional browser storage technologies:

### Service Worker Cache

The Service Worker caches resources for offline access:

- Devotional content and images
- Application assets (CSS, JavaScript)
- Fonts and icons

This cache is stored in your browser and can be cleared through browser settings.

### IndexedDB

We use IndexedDB to store:

- Reading progress and history
- Saved devotionals and bookmarks
- Sync queue for offline changes
- User preferences

### Local Storage

Local Storage is used for:

- Quick-access settings
- UI state (collapsed sections, etc.)
- Temporary data during sessions

## Managing Cookies

### Browser Settings

You can manage cookies through your browser settings:

- **Chrome:** Settings > Privacy and security > Cookies
- **Firefox:** Settings > Privacy & Security > Cookies
- **Safari:** Preferences > Privacy > Cookies
- **Edge:** Settings > Cookies and site permissions

### In-App Settings

Within the EUONGELION app, you can:

- Disable analytics cookies in Settings > Privacy
- Clear local data in Settings > Storage
- Manage notification preferences

### Opt-Out Options

For analytics cookies:

- Use browser Do Not Track (DNT) settings
- Disable analytics in app settings
- Use browser privacy modes

### Effect of Disabling Cookies

Disabling certain cookies may affect functionality:

| Cookie Type | Effect of Disabling        |
| ----------- | -------------------------- |
| Essential   | Cannot use core features   |
| Functional  | Settings not saved         |
| Analytics   | No impact on functionality |
| Performance | No impact on functionality |
