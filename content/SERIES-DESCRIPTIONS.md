**Category:** Onboarding

### How to Read the Bible

**Duration:** 15 days
**Category:** Onboarding / Doctrine
**Audience:** All users seeking foundational understanding

**Tagline:** The Bible isn't a rulebook. It's a story of God pursuing humanity - and it's waiting for you.

**Description:** Learn to read Scripture the way ancient readers did - and the way modern scholars wish more people would. This series teaches you to recognize literary genres, understand historical context, and apply the PaRDeS method of interpretation that Jewish scholars have used for millennia.

**You'll explore:**

- The major literary genres of Scripture (narrative, poetry, prophecy, wisdom, apocalyptic)
- How to read context before content
- The PaRDeS method: Plain meaning, Hints, Application, and Mysteries
- Common interpretive mistakes and how to avoid them

**This series is for you if:**

- You've read the Bible but feel confused by parts of it
- You want to study Scripture more seriously
- You've been taught one way to interpret but suspect there's more
- You want tools that will serve you for a lifetime of reading
