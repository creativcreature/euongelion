01

What is this?

EUONGELION is a daily devotional platform. Each day, you'll receive content designed to help you encounter God through Scripture—not just learn about Him.

We go deeper than most devotionals. We explore the original Hebrew and Greek. We tell stories that make ancient truths tangible. And everything points to Jesus.

Deep, Not Wide

One passage unpacked thoroughly, not ten verses skimmed quickly.

Original Languages

Hebrew and Greek word studies that illuminate what translations flatten.

Response Invited

Breath prayers, reflection questions, and journaling prompts.

03

What to expect

Each devotional takes about 5-10 minutes to read. Here's what you'll find:

Scripture Passage

The text we're exploring, included in full so you don't have to look it up.

Teaching & Exploration

We walk through the passage, illuminating what might be unclear.

Word Studies

Hebrew or Greek terms with pronunciation, meaning, and significance.

Stories

Historical or contemporary narratives that make truth tangible.

Breath Prayer

A short phrase to carry with you—inhale one part, exhale the other.

Reflection Questions

Not quizzes—invitations to honest self-examination.

04

A few notes

You don't have to finish everything. Some days you'll engage deeply. Some days you'll skim. Both are okay. The goal isn't perfection—it's presence.

Day-gating is intentional. One day at a time. This isn't a binge. It's a rhythm. Trust the pace.

This is a marathon, not a sprint. You won't transform overnight. But show up most days for a year? You'll be different.
